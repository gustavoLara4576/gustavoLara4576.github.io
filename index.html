The Seven Kingdoms of Westeros

Westeros is a continent of the universe of Ice and Fire located in the western portion of the world. It's where the gratest parts of the main plots happen in the books and the one we have more infomations about.

The continent is governed by one King, that is the highest authority and have all the people and lords under his orders.

After the King, The lords of the great houses are the highest ranking in their regions and are only vassals to the king. Petty lords are at the bottom of the hierarchy and may only have a village.
